Myths & Facts About HIV/AIDS

Myth

You can get HIV from hugging or touching someone.

Fact

HIV is not spread through casual contact like hugging or sharing food.

Myth

HIV and AIDS are the same thing.

Fact

HIV is a virus, while AIDS is the advanced stage of the infection.

Myth

People with HIV cannot live normal lives.

Fact

With proper treatment, people with HIV can live long, healthy lives.

Myth

You can get HIV from mosquito bites.

Fact

HIV is not transmitted through mosquito bites.
