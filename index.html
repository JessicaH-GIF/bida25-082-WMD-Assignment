Welcome

HealthLink Botswana is a digital health initiative created to improve access to reliable information and support services, with a strong focus on HIV/AIDS awareness. It was established to meet the growing need for accurate, youth-friendly health education in a digital world where many people rely on online platforms for information. The website aims to educate, empower, and connect users to trusted health knowledge through interactive tools, educational content, and accessible support resources such as testing information, counselling services, and hotlines.


Why this website matters

HealthLink Botswana is important because HIV/AIDS remains a major health challenge, especially among young people, and misinformation can increase stigma and risk. By using digital technology, the platform helps spread accurate information quickly, encourages safe health practices, and promotes testing and awareness. It also provides a private and accessible way for users to learn and seek help, contributing to a more informed, supportive, and health-conscious society.

Technology & Awareness

We believe that education is one of the most powerful tools in fighting misinformation and stigma. By combining health education with technology, this platform aims to empower users to make informed choices and contribute to a more knowledgeable and supportive society.
